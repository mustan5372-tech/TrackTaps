# Pod.ai Integration - Quick Start Guide

## 🚀 Getting Started in 3 Steps

### Step 1: Connect to Pod.ai
1. Open TrackTaps: https://www.tracktaps.online
2. Click **Settings** in sidebar
3. Scroll to **Pod.ai Integration** section
4. Enter your Pod.ai email and password
5. Click **"🔗 Connect to Pod.ai"**

### Step 2: View Your Subjects
1. Click **Subjects** in sidebar
2. Your Pod.ai subjects appear automatically
3. Attendance data is populated (present/total/%)
4. Colors are assigned automatically

### Step 3: Check Your Dashboard
1. Click **Home** in sidebar
2. Dashboard shows real Pod.ai data:
   - Overall attendance percentage
   - Safe/critical subject counts
   - Attendance overview
   - Today's schedule

---

## 🔄 Manual Sync

Need to refresh your data?

1. Go to **Subjects** page
2. Click **"🔗 Sync Pod.ai"** button
3. Data updates in real-time

---

## 🔓 Disconnect

To disconnect from Pod.ai:

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Scroll to **Pod.ai Integration**
3. Click **"🔓 Disconnect"**
4. Confirm disconnection
5. Your local data remains saved

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "Authentication failed"
- Check your Pod.ai email and password
- Make sure you're using your Medicaps Pod.ai account
- Try again

### "Failed to fetch attendance data"
- Check your internet connection
- Try clicking "Sync Now" again
- Disconnect and reconnect

### Subjects not appearing
- Make sure you're connected to Pod.ai
- Click "Sync Pod.ai" button
- Refresh the page

### Last sync shows "Never"
- You haven't synced yet
- Click "Sync Pod.ai" button
- Or connect to Pod.ai (auto-syncs)

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Is my data safe?**  
A: Yes! Your credentials are stored securely and never sent to TrackTaps servers.

**Q: Can I use TrackTaps without Pod.ai?**  
A: Yes! You can manually add subjects and track attendance locally.

**Q: What if I disconnect?**  
A: Your local data remains. You can reconnect anytime.

**Q: How often does it sync?**  
A: Auto-sync runs on Home page load. Manual sync anytime via "Sync Pod.ai" button.

**Q: Can I edit Pod.ai subjects?**  
A: Yes! You can edit any subject (including Pod.ai synced ones) in the Subjects page.

**Q: What if Pod.ai is down?**  
A: You'll see an error message. Your local data remains safe.
