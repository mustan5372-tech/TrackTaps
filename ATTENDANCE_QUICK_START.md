# Attendance System - Quick Start Guide

## 🚀 Getting Started in 3 Steps

### Step 1: Add Your Timetable
1. Go to **Timetable** page
2. Click any cell (day + time)
3. Select a subject
4. Subject appears in that slot
5. Repeat for all your classes

### Step 2: Calendar Auto-Generates
1. Your timetable automatically syncs to calendar
2. Calendar shows recurring weekly events
3. Each subject appears every week on the same day/time

### Step 3: Mark Attendance
1. Go to **Calendar** page
2. Click a date
3. Modal shows all scheduled subjects
4. Mark each subject or use bulk controls

---

## 📅 How to Mark Attendance

### Quick Bulk Marking
1. Click a date in calendar
2. Use one of 4 buttons:
   - **✓ All Present**: Mark all subjects as present
   - **✗ All Absent**: Mark all subjects as absent
   - **◯ All Off**: Mark all subjects as off (doesn't count)
   - **Clear All**: Remove all markings

### Individual Subject Marking
1. Click a date in calendar
2. For each subject, click:
   - **✓**: Mark present
   - **✗**: Mark absent
   - **◯**: Mark off
   - **Clear**: Remove marking

---

## 📊 Understanding Attendance States

| State | Icon | Color | Meaning |
|-------|------|-------|---------|
| Present | ✓ | Green | You attended |
| Absent | ✗ | Red | You missed |
| Off | ◯ | Purple | Holiday/Off day (doesn't count) |
| Unmarked | ○ | Gray | Not yet marked |

## 💡 Tips & Tricks

### Bulk Mark Entire Week
1. Click each day in calendar
2. Use "Mark All Present" button
3. Quickly mark entire week

### Mark Off Days
1. Use "Mark All Off" for holidays
2. Off days don't count toward percentage
3. Useful for exam weeks or breaks

### Clear Mistakes
1. Click "Clear" button for individual subject
2. Or use "Clear All" for entire day
3. Attendance recalculates automatically

### Check Subject Status
1. Go to **Subjects** page
2. See each subject's attendance %
3. Color-coded: Green (safe), Yellow (warning), Red (critical)

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Do I need to add events to calendar manually?**  
A: No! Calendar auto-generates from your timetable.

**Q: What does "Off" mean?**  
A: Off days (holidays, breaks) don't count toward your percentage.

**Q: Can I edit attendance later?**  
A: Yes! Click any date and change the marking anytime.

**Q: How is percentage calculated?**  
A: (Present Classes / Total Classes) × 100  
Off days are excluded from total.

**Q: What if I miss marking a day?**  
A: It shows as "Unmarked" in gray. Mark it anytime.

**Q: Does my data save?**  
A: Yes! Everything saves automatically to your browser.

**Q: Can I export my attendance?**  
A: Yes! Go to Subjects page and click "Export Data".
